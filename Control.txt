Mage Academy

Control:
player1 (red, on the left)
w,s,a,d for movement
r,t,y for light magic attack spell,heavy magic attack, summon creature
g,h for generating fire and ice orbs. everything other than light attack require orbs

player2 (blue, on the right)
arrow keys for movement
i,o,p for light magic attack spell,heavy magic attack, summon creature
l,; for generating fire and ice orbs.
